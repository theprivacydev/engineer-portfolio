About Me

I am a Full Stack Web Developer with 3 years of programming experience. Coding JavaScript, problem-solving, and learning are my greatest passions. My technical skillset includes: Git, Heroku, HTML5, CSS3, Saas/Scss, Bootstrap, JavaScript, jQuery, Node.js, Express JS, MySQL, MongoDB, Handlebars.js, React, and Oauth2 authentication. I implement these skills with balance, design, and functionality as my focus.

Intrigued and curious about computer science, I began as a self-taught programmer, and I am known for how quickly I pick up new technologies. I continued my education with the University of Oregon in order to both solidify and expand my stack, and I graduated from their Full Stack Coding Bootcamp in April, 2021. I'm excited to bring my passionate and positive attitude into a fast-paced team of engineers that will allow me to learn by meeting challenges head-on, and collaborating with my colleagues to create solutions built from innovative perspectives.

I am currently studying cybersecurity and educating others on how they can protect their privacy online.
